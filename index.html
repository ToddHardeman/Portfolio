01 · About

Who I Am

From aircraft avionics to AI-powered enterprise systems

Staff Sergeant Todd R. Hardeman serves as a lead Software Project Manager and Avionics Technician for the 660th Aircraft Maintenance Squadron at Travis Air Force Base, California. In this capacity, he directs digital transformation initiatives and manages logistics for a multi-billion-dollar aviation fleet.

A premier innovator within Air Mobility Command, Sergeant Hardeman engineered a hardware solution for Large Aircraft Infrared Countermeasures (LAIRCM) systems projected to avoid over $812 million in annual maintenance costs. He also pioneered the first Unmanned Aerial System (UAS) inspection of the C-17 platform, reducing inspection duration by 92% and establishing an enterprise-wide precedent for automated maintenance.

His expeditionary expertise was forged during a 2019 deployment to Al Dhafra Air Base and a 2025 deployment as Lead Systems Specialist, synchronizing global connectivity assets and secure data-link architectures — including Link-16 operations — in support of national-level security objectives.
