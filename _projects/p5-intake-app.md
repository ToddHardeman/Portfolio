Administrative overhead represents one of the most persistent drains on organizational efficiency. Manual intake processes — receiving requests, routing them to appropriate personnel, tracking status, and maintaining records — consume disproportionate time and introduce significant error risk at scale.

This project co-developed a custom intake application using Microsoft Power Platform to transform that process. Working collaboratively with operational stakeholders, requirements were gathered and translated into a Power Apps interface designed for ease of use by non-technical personnel, backed by Power Automate workflows that route, escalate, and track requests automatically.

MS Lists served as the data backbone, providing structured storage that integrates seamlessly with the broader M365 ecosystem and supports reporting and analytics. The application was designed with scalability in mind — capable of handling high-volume operations at enterprise scale.

The deployment resulted in measurably faster response times, reduced manual effort, improved data integrity, and lowered operational costs — demonstrating how low-code development, when properly architected, can deliver enterprise-grade solutions with remarkable speed.
