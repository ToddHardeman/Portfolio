Large organizations struggle with knowledge fragmentation — critical professional development resources, mentorship frameworks, and operational best practices exist in countless disconnected locations or individual minds, never reaching those who need them most.

This project addressed that challenge head-on. By architecting a centralized knowledge-sharing platform within the Microsoft 365 ecosystem, a unified hub was created for professional development content, mentorship resources, and organizational information that could scale across the Air Force enterprise.

The platform was designed with information architecture principles at its core — ensuring content was discoverable, structured, and maintainable. Implementation involved coordinating with stakeholders across 10 Major Commands to understand diverse needs and ensure the system served all user groups effectively.

The result was a living platform that standardized professional development, improved information flow, enhanced mentorship capabilities, and enabled collaborative learning across a geographically dispersed, 10,000+ member enterprise.
